Story

Through the ages, dueling was traditionally fought out between foes using swords and rapiers, and we have now brought it into the 21st century with a fun interactive game between two phone users.

Following prompts on phone and reacting to the actions of your opponent, you take turns taking jabs at each other to try to get them out of the game!

____ aims to reduce screen time and improve social interactions through two player duels
