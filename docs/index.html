Story

Through the ages, dueling was traditionally fought out between foes using swords and rapiers, and we have now brought it into the 21st century with a fun interactive game between two phone users.

Doul aims to reduce screen time and improve social interactions through two player duels. Let your imagination roam free and fight to the death!

Following prompts on phone and reacting to the actions of your opponent, you take turns taking jabs at each other to try to get them out of the game!

Our Impact

Even though technology has allowed us to be more connected to others, it also has an opposite effect of contributing to social isolationism within some. Video games are an increasingly isolated hobby, often with little positive contact with friends and can result in a lot of stress and shouting in dark rooms.

We aimed to change this with Duol. This game is both active and personal, allowing players to meet face to face and experience a more personal communication than a typical voice or text chat. This helps bring back a bit of humanity back into video games that we seem to have somewhat lost over the years, and hopefully can change people’s playing behaviours for the better.
